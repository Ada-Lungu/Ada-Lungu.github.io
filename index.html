Outdoor activities

Education through outdoor activities!


In collaboration with Outdoor Life, we organize educational weekends and summer camps for youth. One learn self-confidence and inter-communication,critical and strategic thinking, reactions in extreme situations, first-aid and survival techniques through much fun, adrenaline and nature-connected activities.

Support Kenya

Rise up, Africa!


In collaboration with Hope Academy, a primary school in Kibera slum, in Kenya, we give our support to build up a stronger and more efficient education system. We organize Fundraising Events to involve local community to be more responsive to other less enriched societies. See our upcoming events!

Connect

Team Building


We co-organize team-building activities with OutdoorLife in weekends. We want to escape the city life and explore the challenges that arise as a team in a new environment. Teams naturally learn through the set of outdoor activities provided practical cooperation, strategic thinking, optimization of resources.

Documentaries Lovers Club


We love to watch documentaries together. For all lovers of continous learning. For those who are restless in discovering their surounding world. We organize documentaries evenings to do this search of the world together!

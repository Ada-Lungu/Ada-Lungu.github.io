"Omul, cat traieste, tot invatza" sais a romanian proverb. One keeps learning while he is alive. LED is a community of people that believe that life is a constant adventure in which we learn in formal and non-formal ways. We think that we, as individuals, we need to grow and expand our knowledge and domains of expertise continually.

Who are We?

We are a bunch of enthusiast people who live their lives according to the "always learning something new" priciple. WE believe we are in a constant growth. Education in its general sense is a form of learning in which the knowledge, skills, and habits of a group of people are transferred from one generation to the next through teaching, training, or research. Education frequently takes place under the guidance of others, but may also be autodidactic.[1] Any experience that has a formative effect on the way one thinks, feels, or acts may be considered educational. Education is commonly divided into stages such as preschool, primary school, secondary school and then college, university or apprenticeship.

What we believe?

We believe we can share our knowledge with each other in the community. We believe we learn better by doing and by teaching others.

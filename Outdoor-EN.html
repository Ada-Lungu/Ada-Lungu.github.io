The Educational Camp Model

The educational camps we organize have a set of activities which are based on outdoor and adventure activities. They are targeted to stimulate creativity, spontaneity as well as practical thinking and strategic organization. We believe a dynamic natural outset stimulates human cognitive process, making us more alerts and creative. The one-week camp prototype we organize has the final objective of developing a set of skills in participants. It is patterned as an initiatic experience that involve the participants through a set of challenges. The challenges are built up gradually, setting up the framework for developing specific personal abilities.

The set of abilities gained through the program:

Critical Thinking and Problem Solving

Group Fusion and Role Play

Decision Making and Stress Control

Survival and First-Aid
